**Protection**
To claim land, craft a claim anchor. You'll need an ender pearl, 4 logs, and 4 stone for the cheapest one.

Use <dark_aqua>/polydex<>, then search for "anchor" to see all the recipes.

Use <dark_aqua>/goml help<> to see how to add other players to your claim.
[page]
**Flying**
You can craft a <dark_green>[Hang glider]<> to fly without an elytra.

To use a hang glider, hold it in either hand, and right click while falling. Firework rockets don't work with hang gliders.

**Trains**
Sneak and right click a <dark_green>[Minecart]<> with a <dark_green>[Chain]<>, then do the same with another one to create a train! The second cart will follow the first.

Place <dark_green>[Gravel]<> under rails to make carts run at top speed.
